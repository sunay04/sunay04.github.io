# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors are potential clients, collaborators, or hiring reviewers scanning a creative portfolio for taste, range, and confidence.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Sunay's creative work, services, and contact path in a memorable single-page portfolio. Success means the visitor understands the work quickly and feels enough trust to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, atmospheric, precise. The portfolio should feel immersive and crafted without obscuring the work itself.

## Anti-references

Avoid the previous glossy mascot-like 3D decoration, floating 3D objects, generic purple gradients, and template-like portfolio sections. The site should not become a space-travel product landing page; it should remain a portfolio using that visual language.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the portfolio information architecture intact.
2. Let atmosphere frame the work, not replace it.
3. Use liquid-glass surfaces only where they improve legibility or navigation.
4. Prefer calm cinematic motion over busy decorative effects.
5. Keep contact and project exploration obvious from the first viewport.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, readable body copy on dark/video surfaces, keyboard-accessible links, and reduced-motion fallbacks for video and entrance animation.
